Monthly Events!

Succession Events

Monthly events, (Also called Succession Events) have players compete against each other and against players of other factions for the illustrious title of /

These events happen on the first week of every month and last for 7 days. The winner is crowned as the server's new emperor, a title they keep until the next Succession Events.

As emperor you get certain benefits and the ability to give your friends certain benefits too.

Factions vying for power.

Join Arcadius and choose your path: stay factionless for a vanilla experience, or join the Latin or Pirate faction.

Each faction offers unique rewards and items unlocked by meeting specific requirements. Level up faction-specific weapons/tools for enhanced abilities.

Enjoy faction-specific mechanics like Pirates' parrot pets and Latins' enchantment master, Edward. Explore faction locations like "Latium" and "Calavera".

Switch factions at will, but with cooldown and reputation costs.

Dive into the game for more faction lore and functionality details!

Land Claiming

Players can claim land to protect it from griefing.

You can only claim a limited number of blocks, but you can earn more claimblocks by playing on the server and by voting for the server on voting sites.

Learn More  →

Regular Resets!

The server is reset regularly every 8 months.

After each reset, all player progress is reset back to zero and the old world is put up for download.

Players are allowed to keep items of sentimental items (Items that do not give players a head start over others)
